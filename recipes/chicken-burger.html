Chicken Burger

Description

Indulge in a crispy fired chicken burger on soft brioche buns, topped with fresh lettuce, tomato, tangy pickles, and creamy mayo—a mouthwatering delight for any mealtime!

Ingredients

  • 2 x 250g chicken breast or 4 boneless thigh fillets
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1 1/2 tsp cooking salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 brioche buns
  • 2 - 3 cups iceberg lettuce
  • 1 sliced tomatoe (optional)
  • sliced pickles
  • 2/3 cups mayonnaise
  • 3/4 tsp diced garlic
  • 3/4 cup plain flour
  • 1/4 cup corn flour
  • 1/4 tsp celery salt
  • 1/2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp onion poweder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp mustard powder
  • 1/4 tsp ginger powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp cooking salt
  • 1 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 3 cups vegetable oil

Method

  1. Chicken – Cover the chicken with a freezer bag or plastic sheet and pound to 1cm / 0.4" even thickness. Trim to just a touch larger than the size of your bun. Save off-cuts for another use (stir fry – or Dozer, in my case!).
  2. Marinade – Whisk Marinade ingredients in a bowl. Add chicken, toss. Marinate in the fridge for 3 hours minimum, to overnight (don't go beyond 24 hrs).
  3. Garlic mayo – Mix in a small bowl and refrigerate until required.
  4. Prepare crunchy coating – Mix flour, cornflour and Seasoning ingredients in a bowl. Drizzle over 2 tablespoons of the marinade, then use fingers to mix through to form lumps – when this sticks to the chicken, it fries up into awesome crunchy bits!!
  5. Heat oil in a large heavy based pot over medium high heat to 180°C/350°F (Note 2).
  6. Coat chicken – Pick up a piece of chicken and allow excess marinade to drip off. Press to coat in flour coating. Shake off excess, then fry for 2 minutes on each side until golden. DO NOT TOUCH for the first 90 seconds to allow the coating to adhere. I fry 2 at a time. Drain on paper towels, cook remaining chicken.
  7. Assemble – Smear garlic mayo on the top and bottom of the buns. Pile lettuce on the base, top with tomato if using. Then chicken, gherkins and put the lid on. Sink your teeth into it immediately and enjoy!
